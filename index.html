01 · The Problem

Teens are stressed.
Quietly.

Academic pressure, social anxiety, identity questions — teens carry enormous emotional weight but rarely have tools that feel made for them. Most wellness apps are built for adults, are overly clinical, or are lost in gamification gimmicks. Stillwater is different: it creates a judgment-free, minimalist space where teens learn to sit with, understand, and gently work through their emotions.

1 in 3
teens experience significant anxiety or stress during their school years, yet fewer than 20% seek support.
72%
of teens say they wish they had better tools to manage their emotions — but most don't know where to start.
02 · Target Users

Built for them,
not for adults.

Stillwater is designed for 13–21 year olds — specifically those navigating school stress, emotional overwhelm, or simply wanting to understand themselves better.

🎒
The Exam Survivor
Age 15–18 · High School
Drowning in deadlines and self-doubt. Needs quick grounding tools between study sessions and a way to process performance anxiety.
🏠
The Quiet Overthinker
Age 13–16 · Middle / High School
Internalises everything. Never mentions stress at home. Journaling and mood tracking gives them a private, safe outlet they desperately need.
🎓
The New University Student
Age 18–21 · University / College
First time living alone, managing everything independently. Needs gentle daily structure and emotional anchoring during a huge life transition.
03 · Core Features

Five quiet tools.

No overwhelming dashboards. No streaks that shame you. Just five thoughtful features that actually help.

01
🌡️
Daily Mood Check-in
A 10-second morning ritual. Choose a colour, a word, or just a shape that represents how you feel. Over time, Stillwater helps you spot emotional patterns without you having to do the analysis yourself.
Self-awareness
02
🌬️
Breath & Grounding Room
Guided breathing exercises (box breathing, 4-7-8, physiological sigh) with animated visuals synced to your breath. No narration — just visual guidance. Can be used in class, before exams, or at 2am.
Immediate calm
03
📓
Gentle Journal Prompts
Three optional prompts each evening. Not therapy questions — just gentle nudges like "What made today heavier than usual?" or "Name something small that went okay." Entries are private, never shared.
Emotional processing
04
⏱️
Study Break Timer
A Pomodoro-style break reminder that doesn't just pause the clock — it guides you through a 2-minute micro-reset: stretch, breathe, step away. Built for students who forget to stop.
Cognitive recovery
05
📊
Emotion Map · Weekly Patterns
A gentle, visual weekly summary that shows emotional trends — not as alarming charts, but as abstract landscapes. A calm week looks like still water. A turbulent week looks like ripples. No judgment, just insight. Students can optionally share this snapshot with a trusted adult.
Insight · Optional sharing
04 · How It Works

Simple from day one.

No lengthy onboarding. No forced habits. Stillwater meets teens where they are and grows with them gently.

🪨
Meet your Pebble
Name your companion. It starts small and grows as you show up for yourself.
🎯
Set one intention
Not a goal — just a word. "Calmer." "Present." "Less reactive." That's enough.
🌅
Morning check-in
10 seconds. How are you? Colour, word, or emoji. Your Pebble responds warmly.
🌿
Daily micro-moments
Use breathing tools, study timers, or journal when needed — no pressure to do all three.
🌊
Weekly reflection
Your Emotion Map appears. Your Pebble grows. You feel the quiet progress.
05 · Design Language

Calm is a visual choice.

Every pixel is intentional. Stillwater's design philosophy: reduce cognitive load, invite stillness, never alarm.

Colour Palette
Forest deep, sage green, warm stone, and mist. Organic and grounding — inspired by nature, not tech. No alarm reds, no anxiety-inducing blues.
Cormorant
DM Sans Light
Typography
Serif headlines for warmth and humanity. Light sans-serif body text for readability. Never bold headings that feel urgent or clinical.

06 · Unique Feature

Your Pebble.

Not a pet. Not a game character. Something quieter — a living reflection of your inner state.

The Pebble Companion

It grows
when you do.

Your Pebble is a small, abstract stone companion that lives on your home screen. It doesn't talk. It doesn't demand things. But it gently reflects your emotional journey through its shape, colour, and texture — without words.

  • A jagged, rough pebble when stress is high — it softens and smooths over days of self-care
  • Colour shifts from grey-blue to warm sage green as your mood improves across the week
  • Gentle micro-animations: it "breathes" when you open the app, pulses softly after a journal entry
  • No score, no XP, no leaderboard — progress is felt, not measured
  • Optional: share a "pebble snapshot" with a friend or counsellor as a way to show how you've been feeling without having to explain
