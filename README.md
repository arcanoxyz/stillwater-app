# 🪨 Stillwater

> *"Find your still."*

A mental wellbeing app concept designed for teens and students — a calm, minimalist space to understand emotions, manage stress, and build quiet resilience.

---

## ✨ What it is

Stillwater is a single-page app concept built with HTML & CSS. No frameworks, no dependencies — just a thoughtfully designed wellbeing experience for young people who need a quiet corner in a noisy world.

---

## 🌿 Features

- **Mood Check-in** — 10-second daily emotional snapshot
- **Breath & Grounding Room** — animated, silent breathing guides
- **Gentle Journal Prompts** — private evening reflections
- **Study Break Timer** — Pomodoro-style micro-resets for students
- **Weekly Emotion Map** — visual patterns, not alarming charts

---

## 🪨 The Pebble

The heart of Stillwater. A silent companion that lives on your home screen and slowly changes shape, texture, and colour based on your emotional journey. No scores. No XP. Just a quiet reflection of how far you've come.
